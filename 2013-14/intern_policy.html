Internship Policy 2013-14

Policy

The internship policy will be applicable to all the students who apply for internships open through SPO , for the session 2013-14 ,and is to be followed during the entire duration of the session.

Eligibility

  • Only 3rd year Undergraduate or 4th year M.Sc. Integrated students can apply for internships through SPO.
  • Student can register only once during an academic year.

Rules & Regulations

  • As soon as a student receives an internship offer, he/she is no longer eligible to apply for internships through SPO for the rest of the Internship Season.
  • A student must be present during the entire Summer Recruitment Process (including PPTs) of the company, if one has applied for its internship process.Monetary fine of INR 250 shall be imposed on a student who misses the PPT without any genuine reason.
  • If a student withdraws from a company's process (not attending any of the GDs, tests, PI, etc.) after applying (without any genuine reason*), one shall be deregistered from SPO and shall not be able to apply for any Internship through SPO for that session.
  • If a student gets an Internship from anywhere other than the SPO, he must inform the SPO as soon as possible by dropping a mail to sposecy@iitk.ac.in so that all his applications for Internship could be withdrawn.
  • A student who leaves the Internship of SPO before or during the stipulated internship period after getting selected, shall be termed as an Internship Defaulter. An Internship Defaulter shall be subjected to a penalty of being allowed to sit for only two companies in the first week (7 days) of placement season.
  • If a student directly contacts a Company that comes through SPO, then it would be considered as an Off-Campus attempt.
  • If a student gets more than one offer in a single day then he has the option of choosing between them at the end of the day. The choice must be communicated to the SPO after the decision has been made.
  • Discrepancies, false claims or any objectionable content found in the resumes of a student would prevent him/her from taking part in any of the placement activities for that season and make him/her an Internship Defaulter.
    * Genuine reasons include health problems, academic clashes or other reasons subject to approval of Chairman, SPC
