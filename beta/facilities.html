Facilities

  • Pickup services on payment basis from Lucknow Airport and Kanpur Central Railway Station.
  • Fully equipped Seminar and conference rooms for Group discussions and Personal Interviews.
  • Centrally air conditioned Auditorium and Lecture halls, fully equipped with latest multimedia and Wi-Fi for pre placement talks, workshops, tests etc.
  • Facility of Tele Conferencing and Video Conferencing.
  • On campus accommodation in visitors' guest house for visiting recruiters (subject to availability).
  • Access to all student profiles via an highly efficient and effective Placement Automation System (PAS).
  • Round the clock assistantship by the students' volunteers at each level of placement process.
  • Highly motivated and experienced staff to handle the whole process.
