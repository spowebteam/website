Culturals and Extra Curriculars

IIT Kanpur provides an environment that nurtures leaders in addition to sound engineers. Students independently manage several club activities, national level festivals, administration of hostels and thus emerge as winner not only in national level competitions but also in their corporate life as well. This provides a platform to the students to hone their inter-personal skills and provides a holistic education.

The National Festivals of IIT Kanpur budget around 1.5 crore provide a unique opportunity where the students can experience management lessons first hand. Right from the stage of conceptualization to final implementation the students manage everything on their own. The organizing team gets to interact and deal with corporate, celebrities and hence inculcate managerial and inter-personal skills.

Techkriti: The Global techno-entrepreneurial festival of IIT Kanpur attracts the best talent from all across the world in its competitions. Several high profile dignitaries from the field of science and technology also attend the festival.

Antaragni: The festival in its 43rd year has evolved as the biggest cultural festival in North India which witnesses more than 6000 participants. Some of the best known artists in the country from the field of music, dance, theatre and arts have been a part of the festival in the past. A team of more than 600 students works for around nine months to put up the show.

Udghosh: The national level sports festival of IIT Kanpur sees participation from all corners of the country. The festival provides a platform for the participating teams and the organizers to inculcate a sense of team work and leadership.
