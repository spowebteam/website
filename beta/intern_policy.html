Internship Policy 2013-14

The internship policy will be applicable to all the students who apply for internships open through SPO , for the session 2013-14 ,and is to be followed during the entire duration of the session.

Eligibility

  • Only 3rd year Undergraduate or 4th year M.Sc. Integrated students can apply for internships through SPO. 4th year Dual Degree students may be allowed for appearing if they are able to get a No Objection Certificate from their thesis guide at the time of application for the respective internship.
  • Student can register only once during an academic year.
